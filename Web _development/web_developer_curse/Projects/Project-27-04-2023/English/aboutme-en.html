ABOUT ME

Nádia, I was born in 2007 and I live in the beautiful city of Porto, Portugal. I'm a student athlete, at the moment I'm at the end of my 10th year, in the language and humanities course and I play Volleyball at Leixões Sport Club for the cadet and youth levels.

In addition to my parents, I have an older brother who I take as a inspiration for my life, he was always very dedicated and hardworking to achieve what he wanted for his life, he is the person I take always in consideration that it helps me when I have certain doubts or even choices and need advice.

People tend to characterize me as a very communicative person, friendly, committed to the realization of my plans, works and dreams

How do I characterize myself? - As a dreamer, hard worker, perfectionist, cheerful but like all of us I also have my flaws among them stubbornness, being very detail-oriented and a little impatient, but thanks to volleyball I was able to start working on some of these small flaws like the patience we have during a game or training session to maintain so that we can maintain a good pace of play and thinking.

I'm a person who always likes to be doing something, not even that is minimal, that's why I'm a very active person, I really like chat, with friends or family, I listen to music of various genres a lot, I love meeting other musical types, reading is one of my things favorite things to do when I have time and it helps me a lot when it comes to concentration and learning, I especially like to read about novel, fiction, but also informative books about sport or day to day issues.
